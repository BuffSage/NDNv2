Our Specialist Services in Detail

Asbestos Remediation (TRGS 519)

Asbestos is a serious health hazard. We remediate professionally and with certification. We remove, among others:

Asbestos cement panels (Eternit) on roofs and facades
Asbestos-containing pipes, ventilation ducts, and fittings
Floor-Flex tiles and asbestos adhesives
Fire protection claddings, sealants & sprayed asbestos

We work dust-free, safely, and according to regulations – including disposal and documentation.

Mineral Fibers & Hazardous Materials

Artificial mineral fibers and other hazardous substances require professional remediation. Our expert team removes:

Mineral fibers (glass wool, rock wool, slag wool)
PCBs (polychlorinated biphenyls) in sealants and paints
PAHs (polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons) in tar adhesives
Wood preservatives such as PCP, Lindane, and DDT

All work is carried out according to current guidelines with appropriate protective equipment and proper disposal.
